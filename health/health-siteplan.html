Health & Wellbeing Site Plan

Site Name

Health & Wellbeing
This name represents a wellbeing site that focuses on fitness and nutrition with tips to better wellbeing.

Site Purpose

The site provides a variety of information on fitness demonstrations with tips, nutrition and other helpful resources for those who are interested in improving overall health.

Color Schema

The colors I will be using in my web project are neutral with some strong tones.
I will opt for black, white, silver, gold, yellow, blue, and neutral colors.
The black will mainly be used for font color, and the color combinations for each individual page are yet to be decided as I progress into my project.
As I progress into my project I have considered tools such as coolors.co to explore variations of color combinations to use.

Typography

The fonts I will be using are open sans and inter.
I have gone for simple styled fonts as I want my target audience to be able to read my website content clearly.
I will use my fonts for heading, body and footer.
I have contemplated fancier styled fonts for headings but do not want my target audience to get confused with different styles of fonts.

Headings Font - font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;

Normal Text/Paragraph Font - font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;
